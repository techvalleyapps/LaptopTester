💻 Useful Commands
Copy and run in PowerShell or Command Prompt.
Battery report (saves to your profile folder):
powercfg /batteryreport
Energy efficiency diagnostic (~60-second scan):
powercfg /energy
Battery info via PowerShell:
Get-WmiObject Win32_Battery | Select-Object *
Design vs full charge capacity (PowerShell):
(Get-WmiObject -Class BatteryStaticData -Namespace root\wmi).DesignedCapacity
